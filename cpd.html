Continued Professional Development

As part of my education at NTU, I've been asked to keep a journal of extracurricular activities and learning experiences that I've undertaken. This is a collection of those activities.

Python Basics Workshop

I have spent the last year out of education. The student-run DevSoc workshop was a helpful reintroduction into the world of computer science. They provided a platform to get involved and ask question where I wouldn't have in class.

SST Welcome Event

The School of Science and Technology held a welcome event for new students. I attended to meet other students and find out more about the school. I also received information about the employability and careers services offered by NTU.

Bletchley Park

During Digital Horizons week at NTU, I took part in the excursion to Bletchley Park museum. Here I learned of the importance of computer scientists, mathematicians, and linguists during World War II.

Fujitsu Presentation

I had the opportunity to attend a presentation by Fujitsu. They spoke about their work in the field of AI and image recognition. I found their research to be rather unsettling due to the laws involved, but it was interesting nonetheless.
